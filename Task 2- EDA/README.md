# 📊 Task 2 - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence

## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this task is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Superstore Sales dataset, uncover meaningful business insights, answer key business questions, and create an interactive dashboard for decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Excel

---

## 📂 Dataset
Dataset Used: `small_superstore.csv`

The dataset contains information about:
- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Sales
- Profit
- Regions
- Categories

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### 1. Data Exploration
- Loaded and inspected the dataset
- Checked data types and structure
- Generated summary statistics

### 2. Sales Analysis
- Analyzed sales distribution
- Identified sales trends and patterns

### 3. Regional Performance
- Compared sales across different regions
- Identified the best-performing region

### 4. Category Profitability
- Evaluated profit generated by each product category
- Identified profitable and loss-making categories

### 5. Product Performance
- Ranked products based on total sales
- Identified Top 10 products by sales

### 6. Business Insights
- Answered key business questions using data analysis

---

## 📈 Business Questions Answered

### Q1. What are the Top 5 Products by Sales?
Identified the highest revenue-generating products.

### Q2. Which Region Generates the Highest Sales?
Compared regional sales performance.

### Q3. Which Category Generates the Highest Profit?
Analyzed category-wise profitability.

### Q4. Who are the Top 5 Customers by Sales?
Identified high-value customers.

### Q5. What is the Average Sales Value?
Calculated average sales per transaction.
